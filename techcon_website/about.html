Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a coalition of industry leaders and educators seeking to create a space where innovation and knowledge converge. From humble beginnings as a local meetup, it has evolved into one of the most respected technology conferences in the world.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire, educate, and connect professionals in technology. We strive to provide a platform for sharing breakthrough ideas, discovering new tools, and fostering collaborations that shape the future of our industry.
